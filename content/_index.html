Welcome

This webapp will help you learn the New City Catechism’s content. The settings menu (press m to toggle) allows you to change the scripture version and content for your intended audience. You can also dismiss this welcome message in the settings menu.

If you revisit this webapp when you don’t have an internet connection the lessons will still load quickly, but the songs and videos will not load until you regain an internet connection. You can add this webapp to your home screen on mobile devices.

Please start by reading the introduction.
